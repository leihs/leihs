This is direct port of japanese.php

Japanese PDF support.

Usage is as follows:

require ‘fpdf’ require ‘chinese’ pdf = FPDF.new pdf.extend(PDF_Japanese)

This allows it to be combined with other extensions, such as the bookmark module.
